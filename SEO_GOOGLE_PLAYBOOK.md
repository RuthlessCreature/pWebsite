## 2) Launch Week SEO Checklist

1. Verify Google Search Console (Domain property recommended).
2. Submit sitemap: `https://www.pomerol-intl.com/sitemap.xml`.
3. Request indexing for key URLs first:
   - `/`
   - `/products`
   - `/sourcing-service`
   - `/products/industrial-computers`
   - `/contact`
4. Ensure DNS is stable and HTTPS is always on.
5. Keep page titles/descriptions unchanged for at least 2-3 weeks to let Google stabilize indexing signals.

## 3) Backlink Plan (White-Hat, Fastest Safe Path)

### Tier A: Owned links (Day 1-3)

- Company LinkedIn page (About + website link)
- Founder/manager LinkedIn profile (Experience section link)
- YouTube company profile (About link, if available)
- X/Twitter company bio (if used)
- Medium/Substack company profile link (if publishing)

### Tier B: Commercial trust links (Week 1-2)

- Chamber of commerce listing
- Relevant export/import associations
- B2B supplier directories with real company profile
- University/alumni/cross-border community partner pages

### Tier C: Topical authority links (Week 2-4)

- Publish one technical article per week and pitch to:
  - trade blogs
  - industry newsletters
  - partner companies with resource pages
- Suggested article topics:
  - China Supplier Verification Checklist for SME Importers
  - 3C Export Documentation Checklist (Invoice, Packing List, Label Control)
  - How to Reduce BOM Drift in Industrial PC Sourcing

## 4) Anchor Text Mix (Avoid Over-Optimization)

- 60% branded: `Pomerol International`, `Pomerol International Trading (Zhuhai) Co., Ltd`
- 30% partial-match: `China sourcing service`, `industrial computer supplier China`
- 10% generic: `website`, `learn more`, `service details`

## 5) What Not to Do

- Do not buy bulk backlinks.
- Do not use private blog network (PBN) links.
- Do not spam exact-match anchors repeatedly.
- Do not publish thin AI pages only for keyword stuffing.

## 6) 30-Day KPI Targets

- Indexed pages: all core service pages indexed
- Search Console impressions: increasing week over week
- First non-brand clicks from long-tail terms by week 3-4
- Referring domains: +10 to +20 real domains (not spam domains)
